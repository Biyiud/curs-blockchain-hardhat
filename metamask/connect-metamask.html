Connect MetaMask to Your Codespaces Hardhat Network

Before proceeding:

  1. Ensure your Hardhat node is running in your Codespace terminal (npx hardhat node).
  2. Go to the "PORTS" tab in your Codespaces terminal area.
  3. Find the entry for port 8545 (your Hardhat node RPC).
  4. Copy the "Local address" provided by Codespaces for port 8545. It will look something like https://your-codespace-name-####.preview.app.github.dev.
